Before Your Visit

Preparing for Your Appointment

A little preparation helps your visit go smoothly. Here is what every patient should know before arriving.

  • Bring your valid health card
  • Bring your physician's requisition form
  • Arrive a few minutes early
  • Follow any exam-specific instructions
  • Please do not bring valuables
  • Reports are sent to your physician

Abdominal Ultrasound

You may be asked to avoid eating for several hours before your exam so the organs can be seen clearly.

Please confirm preparation instructions with the clinic when booking.

Pelvic & Obstetrical Ultrasound

A full bladder is often required. You may be asked to drink water beforehand and avoid emptying your bladder.

Please confirm preparation instructions with the clinic when booking.

Kidney & Bladder Ultrasound

A full bladder is usually needed so pre- and post-void measurements can be taken.

Please confirm preparation instructions with the clinic when booking.

Frequently Asked

Questions & Answers

Helpful answers about appointments, preparation, and results.

Do I need a requisition?

Yes. A requisition from your physician or healthcare provider is required for diagnostic imaging. You can download our requisition form from this website and bring it with you, along with your valid health card.

How do I book an appointment?

Call us at +1 (905) 215-1815 or use our online appointment request form. We will confirm your time and let you know if any preparation is needed for your exam.

What should I bring?

Please bring your valid health card and your physician's requisition form. Arrive a few minutes early, and follow any exam-specific preparation instructions provided when booking.

How long does an ultrasound take?

Most ultrasound exams take between 20 and 45 minutes depending on the type of scan. We will give you an estimate when you book.

When will my doctor receive results?

Your images are interpreted by qualified professionals and a report is sent to your referring physician. Please follow up with your physician to discuss your results.

Can the technologist tell me my results?

Findings are not discussed by clinic staff. Your results are reported to your referring physician, who will review them with you.

Do I need to prepare for my ultrasound?

Some exams require preparation, such as fasting for an abdominal scan or a full bladder for a pelvic scan. Please confirm preparation instructions with the clinic when booking.

Do you offer obstetrical ultrasound?

Yes. We offer obstetrical ultrasound including dating, 18 to 20 week morphology, and third trimester assessments as requested by your physician.

Do you offer musculoskeletal ultrasound?

Yes. We provide musculoskeletal ultrasound for shoulders, elbows, wrists and hands, hips, knees, ankles, soft tissue, and more.

Where are you located?

We are located at 115 Simcoe St S, Oshawa, ON L1H 4G7, in the heart of downtown Oshawa.

Is parking available?

Parking is available nearby. Please contact the clinic for the most convenient parking options on the day of your visit.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Please call the clinic to confirm availability before visiting, as imaging appointments are typically scheduled in advance.
