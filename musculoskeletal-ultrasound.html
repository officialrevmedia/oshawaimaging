About Oshawa Advance Imaging

A modern, patient-centred diagnostic imaging clinic in the heart of Oshawa, serving Durham Region with professional ultrasound and X-ray services.

Modern Imaging
Who We Are

A Modern Imaging Clinic in Downtown Oshawa

Oshawa Advance Imaging is dedicated to providing professional, patient-centred diagnostic imaging to our community.

Located at 115 Simcoe St S, we offer ultrasound and X-ray services in a clean, comfortable, and welcoming environment. Our focus is simple: reliable imaging, a clear and respectful experience, and timely reporting to your referring physician so your care can move forward without delay.

We proudly serve patients and physicians across Oshawa, Whitby, Courtice, Bowmanville, Ajax, Pickering, Clarington, and the wider Durham Region.

Our Commitment

What Guides Our Care

Quality Imaging

We are committed to producing clear, reliable diagnostic images to support accurate interpretation and reporting.
