How It Works

Your Imaging Visit, Step by Step

Step 1

Get a Requisition

Your physician completes a requisition for the imaging you need.

Step 2

Book Your Visit

Call us or request online. We will confirm timing and any preparation.

Step 3

Attend Your Exam

Bring your health card and requisition. Our team performs your imaging.

Step 4

Results to Your Doctor

A report is sent to your referring physician to review with you.

Not Sure Which Exam You Need?

Your physician will indicate the right imaging on your requisition. If you have questions about your visit, our team is happy to help.
