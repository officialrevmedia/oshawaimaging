Preparing for Your Appointment

Simple guidance on what to bring and how to prepare for each type of examination, so your visit goes smoothly.

Before Your Visit

What to Bring & How to Prepare

A little preparation helps your appointment go smoothly. Here is what every patient should know before arriving for any exam.

  • Bring your valid health card
  • Bring your physician's requisition form
  • Arrive a few minutes early
  • Follow any exam-specific instructions below
  • Wear comfortable clothing
  • Please do not bring valuables
If you may be pregnant, please tell the clinic and the technologist before any X-ray examination.

Need to Confirm Preparation?

Preparation can vary depending on your specific exam. Our team is happy to confirm exactly what you need to do before your visit.

  • Fasting requirements
  • Full bladder instructions
  • What to wear and bring

By Exam Type

Preparation by Examination

Abdominal Ultrasound

You may be asked to avoid eating for several hours before your exam so the liver, gallbladder and surrounding organs can be seen clearly. You may usually take essential medication with a little water.

Please confirm preparation instructions with the clinic when booking.

Pelvic Ultrasound

A full bladder is often required. You may be asked to drink water beforehand and avoid emptying your bladder so the pelvic structures can be clearly imaged.

Please confirm preparation instructions with the clinic when booking.

Obstetrical Ultrasound

Preparation depends on the stage of pregnancy. Early scans often require a full bladder. You may be asked to drink water beforehand and avoid emptying your bladder.

Please confirm preparation instructions with the clinic when booking.

Kidney & Bladder Ultrasound

A full bladder is usually needed so pre and post void bladder measurements can be taken. You may be asked to drink water beforehand.

Please confirm preparation instructions with the clinic when booking.

Musculoskeletal & Soft Tissue

Most musculoskeletal and soft tissue exams require no special preparation. Wear comfortable clothing that allows the area to be easily accessed.

Please confirm preparation instructions with the clinic when booking.

X-Ray

Most X-ray exams require little or no preparation. You may be asked to remove jewellery or clothing with metal in the area being imaged. Tell us if you may be pregnant.

Please confirm preparation instructions with the clinic when booking.

Ready for Your Appointment?

Once you know how to prepare, call us or request an appointment online. We will confirm your visit and answer any questions.
