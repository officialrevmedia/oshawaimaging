Information for Referring Physicians

Reliable ultrasound and X-ray imaging with prompt reporting, supporting physicians and their patients across Durham Region.

For Healthcare Providers

Partnering With Referring Physicians

Oshawa Advance Imaging supports physicians across Durham Region with reliable diagnostic imaging and prompt, clear reporting.

We accept physician requisitions for ultrasound and X-ray examinations. Our goal is to make the referral process simple for your office and comfortable for your patients, with timely scheduling and reports returned to you so you can continue your patient's care without delay.

  • Ultrasound and X-ray imaging
  • Reports sent to the referring physician
  • Timely scheduling for your patients
  • A convenient downtown Oshawa location
