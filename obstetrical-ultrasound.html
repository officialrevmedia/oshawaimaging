Accessibility

Our commitment to a welcoming and accessible experience for all patients and visitors.

Oshawa Advance Imaging is committed to providing a welcoming and accessible experience for all patients and visitors.

We strive to ensure that our services and this website are accessible to people of all abilities. This statement is provided for general information.

Our Commitment

We aim to treat every patient with dignity and respect, and to accommodate individual needs wherever reasonably possible. If you require assistance during your visit, please let our team know.

Website Accessibility

This website has been designed with accessibility in mind, including readable typography, clear structure, keyboard-friendly navigation, and sufficient colour contrast. We continue to work to improve the experience for all users.

Requesting Accommodation

If you have an accessibility need related to your appointment, or feedback about the accessibility of this website, we welcome your input and will do our best to assist.

To request accommodation or share accessibility feedback, please contact us at +1 (905) 215-1815 or info@oshawaadvanceimaging.ca.

This website is for general information only and does not replace medical advice. Please speak with your physician or healthcare provider regarding your medical concerns. Imaging results are interpreted by qualified professionals and sent to your referring physician.
